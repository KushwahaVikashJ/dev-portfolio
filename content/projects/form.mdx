The Form Builder App is a comprehensive platform designed to streamline the process of creating and sharing forms for data collection. Inspired by platforms like Google Forms, the Form Builder App offers a seamless experience for users to design forms, share them, and analyze the collected data.

### Tech Stack

The Form Builder App leverages modern web technologies to deliver a robust and intuitive platform:

- Frontend: Next js, Tailwind CSS, Shadcn, Typescript
- Backend: Prisma as ORM

### Implementation

The Form Builder App provides an easy-to-use interface for creating and managing forms. Users can drag and drop different form fields, customize them, and share the forms to collect information efficiently.

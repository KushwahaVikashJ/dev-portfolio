Teckle is an all-in-one platform designed for freelancers and small teams to efficiently manage their projects, clients, finances, and teams. Inspired by comprehensive solutions like Asana and Trello, Teckle provides an integrated approach to handle all aspects of project management.

### Tech Stack

Teckle utilizes modern web technologies to create a powerful and user-friendly platform:

- Frontend: Next.js, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS

> Project Management Screens.

- Tasks and Checklists: Break down projects into manageable tasks and checklists, ensuring every detail is accounted for and nothing is overlooked.
- Priority, Status, and Due Date: Assign priorities, track the status, and set due dates to keep projects on schedule.
- Project Analytics: Gain insights into project progress, financials, workload, and other crucial metrics for a bird's-eye view of your projects.

2. Client Management

<ImageBlog
  src="/images/projects/teckle/teckle_4.png"

> Client Management Screen.

- Integrated Management: Manage clients alongside projects, associating them seamlessly.
- Business Value Tracking: Track the projects completed with each client and the profit generated.
- Relationship Nurturing: Make informed decisions on client relationships based on key performance indicators (KPIs).

3. Team Management

> Team Management Screen.

- Task Assignment: Allocate tasks to team members across multiple projects and monitor their progress.
- Workflow Organization: Visualize team resources to manage workloads effectively and ensure transparency.
- Permission Assignment: Establish team hierarchy by assigning permissions to team members, ensuring appropriate access levels.

4. Financial Management

  src="/images/projects/teckle/teckle_2.png"

> Financial Management Screen.

- Revenue and PnL Tracking: Monitor financial health, comparing revenue and profit goals year-on-year.
- Projections and Analysis: Set and analyze monthly revenue projections, identifying gaps when goals are exceeded or unmet.
- Client and Project Reporting: Generate report cards for clients and projects to understand business performance.
